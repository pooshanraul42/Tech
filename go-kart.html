Portfolio Details

This is design, validation and assembly of a racing go-kart.

Final Assembly Render

Complete CAD model including chassis, engine, steering, aero, and frame elements rendered to show overall packaging and proportions.

Chassis CAD Model

SolidWorks-modeled frame using circular tubing, optimized for torsional rigidity, machining, and manufacturability.

Mesh Overview

Tetrahedral mesh applied with refinement at welds and suspension nodes. Quality metrics ensured numerical stability across all analysis cases.

Front Impact

Stress concentration observed near the front member interface. Design modifications based on simulation resulted in improved absorption and deformation behavior.

Rear Impact

FOS map shows safe margins maintained near drivetrain mounts. Bracing sections confirmed effective through FEA validation.

Torsional Rigidity

Twist measured from diagonal torque test. Deflection minimized across the center plane and front support rails.

Modal Frequencies

Chassis exhibits primary natural frequencies at 59Hz and 117Hz. Design ensured separation from operating frequencies of the drivetrain and suspension vibration zones.

Detailed Analysis – Front Impact

Full front impact simulation results showing load application, stress zones, factor of safety, and final deformation response.

Detailed Analysis – Rear Impact

Rear impact zone validation and response, showing energy absorption at the engine bay and surrounding cross members.

Detailed Analysis – Torsional Test

Comprehensive torsional rigidity test, highlighting twist resistance across diagonally loaded nodes.

Detailed Analysis – Modal Frequencies

Modal analysis highlighting chassis resonance modes and frequencies beyond operational vibrations.
