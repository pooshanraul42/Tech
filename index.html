About

I’m a mechanical engineer focused on simulation-backed mechanical design, experimental validation, and delivering designs optimized for fabrication and assembly. My skillset spans CAD, FEA, CFD, and CAM — backed by hands-on testing and manufacturing experience. I’ve worked on racing chassis, pressure vessels, thermal systems, and automotive interiors. Whether modeling stress behavior or refining cut geometry, I approach problems with a balance of analytical rigor and practical execution.
