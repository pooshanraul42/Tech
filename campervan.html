Modular Campervan Furniture Kits – Design to Manufacturing & Installation

Custom-designed modular furniture systems according to customer requirements. 3D modeled and optimized for DFM and DFA using SolidWorks and VCarve Pro.

I work on the development of configurable modular furniture kits for various campervan platforms, including VW T6s and Crafters, Ford Transit Customs, and Mercedes-Benz Vitos and Sprinters. This project involves full-cycle design in SolidWorks, including geometric features, fastener integration, and panel-level design. I also generate CNC toolpaths in VCarve Pro and optimize each design for material efficiency, ease of assembly, and machine compatibility.

VW Crafter MWB Kit

Tailored for high-utility camper builds, this large-format kit supports full-time vanlife with integrated kitchens, storage, and dual pull-out beds—streamlining onsite installation while maximizing interior mobility.

  • 15mm birch ply modeled for CNC manufacturing
  • Assembly via cam-and-pin connectors with tolerance relief
  • Modular subassemblies allow components to be swapped per customer spec

While each kit is unique in size and features, all are designed for simple and intuitive assembly. This ensures quick modifications during wiring, plumbing, and future maintenance.

  • Cam-and-pin fasteners allow sturdy, non-destructive assembly
  • Fastener locations are optimized to prevent ply delamination and maximize structural lifespan
  • Internal cuts (doors, catches) are pre-machined within each part to save time and material

VW T5.6 LWB Kit

Built for families and long-haul travelers, this extended layout includes sleeping for four and a full-length kitchen—ensuring space efficiency without compromising comfort.

  • Parts nested for 2440×1220 mm plywood with minimal waste
  • Electrical modules isolated for access and safety
  • Three configurable packages: Bronze, Silver, Gold (varying features & price)

Designed to support off-grid use, this package includes water storage solutions, a fully featured kitchen, and the ability to integrate appliances like diesel heaters and stoves. Storage and usability were balanced against compactness.

  • Dedicated compartments for water tanks and utilities
  • Kitchen pre-routed for microwave, gas locker, and sink
  • Off-grid options: solar, diesel heating, and 12V electronics

VW T5.6 U-Shape Kit

Designed for campers seeking maximum comfort in compact vans, this U-shaped layout combines seating, kitchen, and sleeping zones—reducing the need for overhead cabinets and enabling a full bed setup with no extra hardware.

  • Removable center table converts to bed support using slotted guides
  • Integrated kitchen top with sink and hob provision
  • Curved access cutouts and radiused corners for user comfort

Built around a self-contained storage system, this layout maximizes floor-level utility without relying on overhead cabinets. Every volume is engineered for multi-use, efficient routing, and ergonomic installation.

  • Under-seat storage with integrated hinge and catch cutouts
  • Side step built into cabinetry for expanded usable space
  • Optional L-shaped kitchen top upgrade to increase work surface area

Toolpath Generation in VCarve Pro

Each design was exported as 2D DXF from SolidWorks and prepared for machining in VCarve Pro. I assigned pocketing, profiling, engraving, and drilling operations with feed and speed settings optimized for clean edges and short cycle times.

  • Compression bits used to avoid tool changes between upcut/downcut
  • Part tags and engraving embedded to reduce labeling errors
  • Optimized layout and nesting parameters to reduce material wastage

End-to-End Design Workflow

Each campervan kit is developed through a standardized and repeatable pipeline:
Concept → Parametric CAD in SolidWorks → DXF Export → Toolpath Setup in VCarve Pro → Manufacturing → Installation / Flat Pack Delivery

This approach ensures that each design is production-ready, accurately dimensioned, and optimized for rapid installation—whether flat-packed or pre-assembled.
